## Permitted uses (no permission needed)

You may, without asking us:

1. **Refer to nlqdb factually.** "Built with nlqdb", "Compatible with
   nlqdb", "Imports the @nlqdb/sdk package" — nominative fair use.
2. **Reproduce the unmodified Mark in unmodified copies of the
   software** (e.g., the wordmark in our README, CLI banner, or web
   UI), as required by the FSL redistribution clause.
3. **Use the Mark in articles, blog posts, talks, and academic
   papers** that discuss, review, or teach nlqdb.
4. **Use the Mark in package metadata** that identifies a true peer
   dependency on nlqdb (e.g., `peerDependencies: { "@nlqdb/sdk": "*" }`).

## Uses requiring permission

You must obtain written permission from us before:

1. Using **nlqdb** or any confusingly similar mark in the name of
   your product, service, company, domain, social handle, package,
   repository, container image, or organization. Examples we will
   refuse: `nlqdb-pro`, `nlqdb-cloud`, `nlqdbplus.com`, `@nlqdb-team`
   on social platforms, `nlqdb` Docker images you publish, PyPI/npm
   packages prefixed `nlqdb-` that you do not own.
2. Using the **nlqdb logo** in modified form, in merchandise, or in
   any context that suggests endorsement or partnership.
3. Distributing a modified build of nlqdb under the name "nlqdb" or

4. Selling commercial services *named* after nlqdb (consulting,
   hosting, training). You may, however, offer such services if you
   describe them factually as "nlqdb consulting" or "training in
   nlqdb" without using nlqdb as the name of your service.

To request permission, email `trademarks@nlqdb.com`.

## Acceptable usage style

When you use the nlqdb mark in writing:

- **Spell it lowercase**: `nlqdb`, not `NLQDB` or `NlqDB`. (When at
  the start of a sentence, you may capitalize: "Nlqdb is…", but
  lowercase is preferred.)
- **Treat it as a proper adjective**, not a noun or verb: "the nlqdb
  engine" or "nlqdb queries", not "an nlqdb" and not "to nlqdb a
  database". This protects the mark from becoming generic.
- **Do not pluralize or possessive-ize**: not "nlqdbs", not "nlqdb's
  behavior" (use "nlqdb behavior").
- **Use the ™ symbol** on the first or most prominent occurrence in
  your document: `nlqdb™`. Once we register the mark in your
  jurisdiction we will publish an updated policy permitting `nlqdb®`.

## Reporting misuse

If you see someone using the nlqdb name in a way that confuses users
about the source of a product or service, please email
`trademarks@nlqdb.com`. We particularly want to hear about: package
squatting on PyPI, npm, crates.io, or Docker Hub; lookalike domains;
phishing sites styled as nlqdb; and unauthorized commercial
redistributions.
