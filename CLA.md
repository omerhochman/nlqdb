By replying to the comment posted by our CLA bot on your first pull

on behalf of yourself, and (c) authorise us to record your signature
together with your GitHub username, the timestamp, and the commit
SHA of the pull request as evidence of agreement. Electronic
signature is enforceable in Switzerland under
[ZertES](https://www.fedlex.admin.ch/eli/cc/2016/752/en), in the
European Union under the
[eIDAS Regulation](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32014R0910),
in the United Kingdom under the Electronic Communications Act 2000,
and in the United States under
[E-SIGN](https://www.fdic.gov/regulations/laws/rules/6500-3170.html)
and UETA.

You sign once. The CLA covers all of your future Contributions to
the Project until you withdraw your agreement in writing.
