# Support

Thanks for using nlqdb. Here's where to go depending on what you need.

## Bugs and feature requests

Open an issue: https://github.com/nlqdb/nlqdb/issues. Include:

- What you tried (commands, code snippets, URLs).
- What you expected to happen.
- What actually happened (error message, screenshot, response body).
- Your environment (OS, runtime version, package version).

For the smallest possible repro, paste a minimal failing example as a
`<details>` block in the issue. Tiny repros get fast attention; "it
doesn't work in my app, here's a screenshot" tends to bounce.

## Questions and discussion

Open a discussion: https://github.com/nlqdb/nlqdb/discussions. Use
this for "how do I…", "is this the right approach for…", or "what's
the roadmap for…".

We aim to answer or triage discussions within 5 business days. We may
not always have an answer; we'll tell you when that's the case rather
than letting threads die.

## Security

**Do not open a public issue for security bugs.** See
[SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) for the disclosure path
(`security@nlqdb.com`, GitHub Private Vulnerability Reporting, or
Signal on request).

## Commercial / licensing / partnership

Email `hello@nlqdb.com`.

For commercial licensing terms (e.g. enterprise dual-licensing for
customers who can't use [FSL-1.1-ALv2](./LICENSE) directly), email
`licensing@nlqdb.com`.

For trademark questions, see [TRADEMARKS.md](./TRADEMARKS.md) or email
`trademarks@nlqdb.com`.

## What we don't offer (yet)

nlqdb is pre-PMF. We do **not** offer:

- Paid support contracts.
- SLAs of any kind.
- Phone support.
- Real-time chat (no Slack/Discord/Intercom).
- Per-customer onboarding.

When we add any of these, they will be announced here and on the
[changelog](./CHANGELOG.md). Until then: GitHub issues and
discussions are the channels, and we read every one.
